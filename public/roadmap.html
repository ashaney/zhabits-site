zHabits Roadmap

We're excited about the future of zHabits and have big plans to enhance your habit-tracking experience. Here's what we're working on:

  1. Visualizations:
    • Intuitive charts to help you visualize your progress
    • Detailed statistics to give you insights into your habit formation
  2. Customization:
    • Weekly and custom interval habit tracking
    • Personalized habit categories
  3. Reminders:
    • Set custom reminders to keep you on track with your habits
  4. Milestone Celebrations:
    • Achievements and badges to celebrate your successes
  5. Social Features:
    • Option to connect with friends for mutual encouragement (with privacy controls)
  6. Advanced Analytics:
    • Deeper insights into your habit patterns and trends
  7. Habit Stacking:
    • Tools to help you build and manage habit stacks
    • Guidance on creating effective habit chains

We're committed to making zHabits the best tool for your personal growth journey. Stay tuned for these exciting updates!
